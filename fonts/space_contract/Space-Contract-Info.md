# Space Contract
Space Contract is a 16pt pixel art font designed to be as clean, and simple as possible while still evoking a futuristic feeling in the reader.

## Author
[VEXED](https://v3x3d.itch.io/)

## Basic Info
- Base Glyph Height: 16px
- Scales: 16, 32, 48, 64, etc
- Ascender Max: 9px
- Descender Max: 5px

## Kerning
``
c f -2
abgnB36 t -1
cksCELR t -2
T tf -3
RSCBEGK T -2
+- 1 -3
``
